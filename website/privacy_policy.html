Basetao's Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Our Privacy Statement explains how we handle your personal information when you use our Website. We collect and use your information solely for its intended purpose, keep it confidential, and use the latest encryption technologies to protect it.

Information collected

The information we collect about you on this Website may vary depending on your usage. We collect information from electronic forms and email messages you send us.

Like many websites, we collect general information such as the date and time of your visit, pages viewed, and downloads. This information is solely used for statistical, reporting, and website administration purposes. It does not personally identify you. Retargeting Policy

When you visit our website, Basetao.com collects non-personally identifiable information about your activities, such as the content and products you view and purchase. We use this information to show you more relevant advertisements through a process called "Retargeting."

To opt-out of Retargeting, you can either use Google's Ads Settings or delete the cookies in your browser. This option is typically found in the Settings or History tab.
